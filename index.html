Transform your PC into an internet TV

Centralize and integrate your internet tv streams to your windows 10 start screen.

How to use STRMR

1. Install STRMR from Windows Store on your Windows 10 computer.

2. Click on a compatible tv channel link to add the channel to your computer with STRMR.

Example of channel link : France 24

Or click on a compatible tv package link to add a series of channels to your computer with STRMR.

Example of channel link : Example package 

3. Open ‘strm’ link with STRMR when prompted by windows 10.

Channel should be added to your STRMR app. You can now open it from STRMR or pin it to your start page and open it directly from Windows 10.

Notes :

  • To delete a channel from your channel list, right click on the icon.
  • STRMR does not provide channel links, anybody is welcomed to create links to iptv streams and share them (create your own lists !)

Create a channel link

The format of a channel link is :

strm:ChannelName=XXXXX&LogoUri=YYYYY&StreamUri=ZZZZZ

Where XXXXX is the channel name as to be displayed in STRMR and Windows 10 when pinned, YYYY is a link to a logo (.png or .jpg, square format) and ZZZZZ is the link to the IPTV stream. To test your link, you can open it with VLC (vlc.org)

Example of a href link :

<a href=“strm:ChannelName=France 24&LogoUri=https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/6/65/FRANCE_24_logo.svg/1024px-FRANCE_24_logo.svg.png&StreamUri=http://f24hls-i.akamaihd.net/hls/live/221192-b/F24_FR_LO_HLS/master_1200.m3u8”>France 24</a>

Create a package link

The format of a link is :

strm:ChannelListName=XXXX&ChannelListUrl=YYYYY

Where XXXXX is the channel list name as to be displayed in STRMR and  YYYY is the url link.

Example of a href link :

<a href=“strm:ChannelListName=France&ChannelListUrl=http://hastebin.com/raw/aparedogaf”>France</a>.

Database format : 

ChannelID;ChannelName;Language;Country;Type;LogoUri;StreamUri;
